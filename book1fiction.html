A Tale of Two Cities

Book Description

A Tale of Two Cities is a historical fiction novel published in 1859. Charles Dickens set the story against the background of the French Revolution in the late 18th century. It’s a political and romantic tale playing out in the cities of London and Paris as the violent uproar of the revolt goes on. The plot of the story revolves around two men, Charles Darnay and Sydney Carton, who look alike but come from different backgrounds. Darnay is a wealthy French aristocrat who has renounced ties with his family, while Carton is a self-pitying, pessimistic English barrister. They both happen to fall in love with the same woman, Lucie Manette. The story very intricately weaves together their lives and of others over a period of 17 years against a social upheaval.
